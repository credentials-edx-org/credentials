About edX

edX offers interactive online classes and MOOCs from the world’s best universities, including MIT, Harvard, Berkeley, University of Texas, and many others. edX is an online initiative created by founding partners Harvard and MIT.

About the edX MicroMasters credential

An edX MicroMasters credential signifies that the learner has completed a group of courses that add up to a rich understanding of an area of study. Completion of MicroMasters Programs requires that learners earn Verified Certificates from each of the courses included in the Program.
